
Slide 5: Haseeb's Interaction with the Tester
"Haseeb ne Tester ke kaam ko dekha aur unse insights liye:
Responsibilities:

Testers ka kaam hota hai ke software ko dono functional aur non-functional requirements par test karna.
Test cases develop karte hain aur bugs fix karne ke liye developers ke sath collaborate karte hain.
Selenium tool se automated testing karte hain jo regression testing ko fast aur reliable banata hai.
JIRA software ka use hota hai task tracking aur sprint organization ke liye.
Challenges:

Tight deadlines ki wajah se thorough testing kabhi kabhi nahi ho pati.
Har platform ke unique requirements ko adapt karna time-consuming hota hai.
Learnings:

Testing ke Importance: Requirements ke sath aligned test cases issues ko jaldi pakad lete hain.
Collaboration: Testers aur developers ke beech close communication issue resolution fast banata hai.
Adaptability: Testing strategies har platform ke liye evolve karni padti hain.
Cross-Platform Compatibility: Devices aur operating systems ke liye testing ka bohot importance hai."
Slide 6: Hassan's Interaction with the UI Designer
"Hassan ka interaction UI designer ke sath tha, aur unke kaam ko samajhne ka moka mila:
Responsibilities:

UI designer ka kaam hai user-friendly aur accessible interfaces design karna.
Yeh ensure karte hain ke design visually appealing aur functional ho.
Developers ke sath collaborate karke designs ko implement karte hain.
Tools and Design Approach:

Figma tool ka use hota hai designs banane ke liye.
Accessibility guidelines, jaise WCAG, ko follow kiya jata hai.
User-centered design approach, jisme users ki zarurat pehle rakhi jati hai.
Challenges:

Kabhi kabhi stakeholders ka conflicting feedback milta hai, jo design decisions mushkil banata hai.
Visual appeal aur functionality ke beech balance karna ek challenge hota hai.
Learnings:

User-Centered Design: Har decision mein user ki zarurat ko priority dena.
Visual Storytelling: Designs ko intuitive aur engaging banane ka tareeqa.
Collaboration: Feedback loops ke through iterative improvements karna."
